How to shortlist a car in Singapore (without overpaying)

This is a practical shortlist framework: start with your use-case, then control depreciation risk before you obsess over features.

Decision checklist:

  • Pick the smallest category that fits your real needs (size inflation is expensive).
  • Prioritise depreciation per year and reliability; features are secondary.
  • Run a stress test: can you still comfortably pay if insurance/repairs are higher than expected?
