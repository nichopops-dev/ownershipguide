True Monthly Cost of Owning a Car in Singapore (2026 Breakdown)

Last updated: February 2026

Most people underestimate car ownership because they only look at the instalment. The real monthly cost includes depreciation, insurance, fuel, maintenance, parking, and the opportunity cost of capital.

1. The Monthly Cost Categories

  • Depreciation
  • Loan interest
  • Insurance
  • Fuel
  • Maintenance & repairs
  • Parking & ERP
  • Opportunity cost of upfront capital

Ignoring any one of these creates a distorted number.

2. Depreciation (The Biggest Hidden Cost)

In Singapore, depreciation is often the largest component.

Example: If a car costs $150,000 and its projected value after 5 years is $80,000, the depreciation is:

$70,000 over 5 years → ~$1,167 per month

Many drivers never explicitly calculate this.

3. Loan Interest

If financing is used, interest increases total ownership cost. Depending on loan size and rates, this can add:

$200–$600 per month

4. Insurance

Typical comprehensive insurance:

$1,500–$2,500 per year → ~$125–$210 per month

Driver age, claim history, and car type matter.

5. Fuel

Fuel depends heavily on mileage.

Example: 1,200–1,800 km per month can translate to:

$250–$450 per month

6. Maintenance & Repairs

Routine servicing, tyres, wear-and-tear:

$100–$250 per month (averaged)

Unexpected repairs increase volatility.

7. Parking & ERP

Season parking + workplace parking:

$150–$350 per month

ERP depends on routes used.

8. Opportunity Cost

If you commit $40,000 upfront and assume a 5% annual return elsewhere:

Opportunity cost ≈ $2,000 per year → ~$167 per month.

This is rarely considered but financially real.

9. Realistic Monthly Range

Category Estimated Monthly Cost (SGD)
Depreciation $1,000 – $1,400
Loan Interest $200 – $600
Insurance $125 – $210
Fuel $250 – $450
Maintenance $100 – $250
Parking & ERP $150 – $350
Opportunity Cost $100 – $200
Total Estimated Monthly Cost $1,925 – $3,460+

10. Compare With Ride-Hailing

If your total monthly ownership cost approaches $2,000–$3,000, ride-hailing may be cheaper depending on usage.
