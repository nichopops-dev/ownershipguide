Rental property ownership cost: what people forget

Many landlords model only mortgage vs rent. Real ownership cost includes vacancy, maintenance, tax, agent fees, and ‘time cost’.

Decision checklist:

  • Model vacancy as a probability, not a surprise (e.g., 1–2 months every couple of years).
  • Budget maintenance and replacement cycles (aircon, appliances, repainting).
  • Don’t ignore transaction friction: agent fees, legal, tax, and time-to-lease.
