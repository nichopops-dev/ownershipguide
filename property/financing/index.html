Property financing

In Singapore, the loan decision is rarely “lowest rate”. It’s about constraints (TDSR/MSR), cash shock (downpayment + duties + renovation), and exit risk (fees, CPF accrued interest, SSD).

Start with: borrowing limits (TDSR/MSR) → Run affordability stress test →

Recommended path (10 minutes)

  1. Know your ceiling: TDSR/MSR + MSR applies for HDB/EC.
  2. Pick the structure: fixed vs floating → refinance vs reprice.
  3. Stress-test fragility: rate shocks + cash shock + exit costs (CPF + fees).

Common traps

  • Ignoring cash shock: downpayment + BSD/ABSD + renovation drains buffer.
  • Optimising the rate only: you win 0.3% but lose when rates spike or income dips.
  • Forgetting CPF accrued interest: net sale proceeds can be much lower than expected.
  • Exit blindness: fees/SSD + timing can make “upgrade plans” fragile.
