Property Cost in Singapore (2026)

Cluster Overview · Last updated: February 2026

Property ownership in Singapore is a leveraged capital allocation decision. The drivers are not just mortgage instalments — they are interest sensitivity, liquidity lock-up, buyer/seller friction, and exit risk.

Start Here

Recommended path (10 min): Pillar → pick your branch → stress test affordability. If you may sell within 5 years, treat exit friction as the main risk.
