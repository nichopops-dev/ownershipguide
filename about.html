About Ownership Guide

Structured Cost Models for Singapore (2026)

Ownership Guide is a financial modelling project focused on the real cost of ownership in Singapore.

Most ownership decisions are evaluated using visible monthly payments. We model:

  • Total capital exposure
  • Depreciation
  • Liquidity impact
  • Opportunity cost
  • Structural risk

Our Objective

To provide structured, transparent breakdowns that allow individuals to make informed high-commitment financial decisions.

Scope

Current focus areas:

  • Car ownership in Singapore
  • Property ownership in Singapore
  • Comparative capital allocation decisions

Singapore is the starting dataset. The framework is expandable.
