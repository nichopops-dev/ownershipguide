About Ownership Guide

Structured Ownership Cost Models for Singapore (2026)

Ownership Guide is an independent financial modelling website focused on analysing the real cost of ownership in Singapore.

Many high-commitment financial decisions — such as buying a car or property — are evaluated using visible monthly payments. We instead model the full economic exposure.

What We Analyse

Each breakdown focuses on:

  • Total capital exposure
  • Depreciation impact
  • Liquidity commitment
  • Opportunity cost of capital
  • Volatility and structural risk
  • Break-even comparisons (where applicable)

The goal is clarity — not persuasion.

Current Focus Areas

  • Car ownership in Singapore
  • Ride-hailing vs ownership comparisons
  • Property ownership structures
  • Capital allocation trade-offs

Singapore is the starting dataset due to its unique cost structures (e.g. COE, financing rules, stamp duties). The modelling framework itself is scalable.
