How to use the upgrade planner

This planner is meant to help you avoid the most common upgrade mistake: deciding based on a “nice monthly instalment” while ignoring timing risk, cash gaps, and sale/purchase friction.

  • Step 1: estimate conservative sale proceeds for your current home (after fees and CPF refund where applicable).
  • Step 2: estimate the full upfront cash needed for the next home (downpayment, BSD/ABSD if any, legal/agent, renovation buffer).
  • Step 3: check timing: if completion dates don’t align, you may need bridging or temporary housing.

Treat outputs as planning ranges. Your actual eligibility, valuation, and CPF balances drive the real numbers.
