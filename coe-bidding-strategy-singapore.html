COE bidding: how to think about it

COE feels like a ‘game’, but your biggest lever is not tactics — it’s setting a max budget and aligning it to depreciation you can tolerate.

Decision checklist:

  • Define your max all-in monthly cost first, then back-solve the max COE you can afford.
  • Treat ‘winning the bid’ as secondary; avoid paying above your planned depreciation threshold.
  • If you need a car by a deadline, budget for that urgency — scarcity pricing is real.
