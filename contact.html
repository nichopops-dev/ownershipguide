What You Can Contact Us About

  • Data corrections or updates
  • Clarifications on modelling assumptions
  • Collaboration or media enquiries
  • Technical issues on the website
  • General feedback on content clarity

Important Note

Ownership Guide provides structured educational models and decision frameworks. We do not provide personalised financial, legal, tax or investment advice.

If you are making a high-commitment financial decision, you should conduct independent due diligence and consult a licensed professional where appropriate.

What to include in your message

  • The page URL
  • What you expected vs what you saw
  • Screenshot (optional but helpful)

For how we maintain calculators and updates, see Editorial Policy. For ads and sponsorship disclosure, see Advertising Disclosure.

OwnershipGuide.com is operated as an independent digital publication based in Singapore.

What you can contact us about

  • Corrections: broken links, incorrect assumptions, outdated rates, or calculation edge cases.
  • Feedback: which tools you want next, what’s confusing, what would make decisions easier.
  • Partnerships / data sources: if you have official or primary sources we should reference.

How we handle corrections

We treat this site as a planning engine. When you report an issue, we verify against primary sources where possible (e.g., CPF, IRAS, HDB, MAS/LTA) and ship a versioned update. If a change is material, we add a note to the page.
