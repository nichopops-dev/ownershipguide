Last updated: February 2026

Ownership Guide (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates https://ownershipguide.com (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) Information We Collect

We collect two broad categories of information:

A) Information you provide (optional)

If you contact us by email, you may provide personal information such as your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to your enquiry.

B) Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site, we may collect non-personal information such as:

  • Browser type and device type
  • Approximate location (e.g., country/city-level)
  • Referring page and pages visited
  • Time spent on pages and interaction events
  • IP address (processed by providers; we do not attempt to identify you)

2) How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Operate and maintain the Site
  • Understand how content is used and improve clarity/usability
  • Measure performance of pages (e.g., which articles help users most)
  • Support advertising and prevent fraud/abuse (where applicable)
  • Respond to messages you send us

3) Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. Google Analytics may use cookies and similar technologies to collect and process usage data.

Note: Analytics is used to understand aggregate trends (e.g., page popularity), not to identify individual visitors.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to this and/or other websites.
  • Google’s use of advertising cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this Site and/or other sites.

You can control or opt out of personalised advertising using Google’s settings: Google Ad Settings.

Depending on your region, you may also see a consent banner that lets you manage cookie/ad preferences.

5) Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We (and third-party providers) may use cookies to:

  • Enable analytics and measure site performance
  • Support advertising and frequency capping
  • Help detect and prevent abuse or fraud

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that some features may work less reliably if cookies are disabled.

The Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. Please review their privacy policies separately.

7) Data Retention

We keep analytics data only as long as needed for measurement and improvement. If you email us, we keep emails only as long as needed to respond and for reasonable record-keeping.

8) Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
